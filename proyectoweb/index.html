Sobre nosotros/as

Nosotros/as queremos ayudar a nuestros/as lectores/as y miembros de la comunidad a vivir a través de la escrituras. Escribir es un trabajo duro y deberías ser recompensado/a por tu esfuerzo y dedicación.

Crea, inventa y emociona.

Cartéate founder Some One acted upon his dream of launching media outlets under the Nao Media umbrella. But it wasn’t until he was introduced to the teachings of Brandon Gaille from The Blog Millionaire Podcast that he truly learned how to connect the science of keyword research and SEO with the art of writing and communication. Going through Brandon’s course inspired Some to launch InspireFirst. InspireFirst exists to help bloggers and authors like you to use your craft to inspire others and generate passive income.

Cartéate teaches how to earn a passive income from your writing by teaching you how to write and publish helpful articles on your blog and/or publishing a book. Passive income accrues while you do other important things like spend time with family and friends or serve in your community.
